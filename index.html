About Us

M/S Amar Shaw is a trusted agri-business enterprise based in Bardhaman, engaged in paddy procurement, rice milling, husk supply, and rice oil distribution. We work closely with farmers and buyers to ensure fair pricing, quality products, and reliable supply.

Our business is built on transparency, long-term relationships, and a deep understanding of agricultural markets.

Our Core Activities

Paddy Buyer

We purchase paddy directly from farmers, ensuring the best market price and timely payments.

Rice Wholesaler

Supplying quality rice in bulk quantities to traders, wholesalers, and institutional buyers.

Husk Supplier

Consistent supply of rice husk for industrial, boiler, and fuel applications.

Oil Distributor

Distribution of rice bran oil with assured quality and reliable logistics.
